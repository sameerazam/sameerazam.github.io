Capitalism and the world

Capitalism is an economic system based on the private ownership of the means of production and their operation for profit.

Critics of capitalism argue that it concentrates power in the hands of a minority capitalist class that exist through the exploitation of the majority working class and their labor, prioritizes profit over social good, natural resources and the Environment.
