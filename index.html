Hi there. I am Sameer.
Welcome to my Blog.
I write on issues faced in our daily life.
In this era of modernisation, I think it
is very unsustainable to use paper for literary works.
As someone said;
"Humans are the only species that cut trees,
make paper, and write 'SAVE TREES' on it."
So here I am, totally online,
disseminating life experience with aim to improve life of others.
